About Me
After finishing my studies in biology, I realized that a more creative profession would suit me better, so I decided to become a front-end developer.​
I started with HTML, CSS and creating static websites, then I learned how to use JavaScript and DOM for making them dynamic. To practice coding, I solved numerous programming exercises on Edabit.
After getting familiar with these technologies, I moved to React. For me, the most effective way of learning React was learning-by-doing, so I started a reference project: an interactive cookbook application. In the project, I used Bootstrap for styling and making the layout responsive.​
The cookbook application and my Edabit solutions are available on GitHub.​
Besides my technical abilities, I am an effective team player. I always focus on results and understand the importance of collaboration and open communication.​
